## How to obtain Donator Perks

Donator perks can be purchased either through
[**Discord's store page**](https://discord.com/application-directory/1377953187176251564/store) or
[**Patreon**](https://www.patreon.com/notiku).

<Warning>
  **iOS** and **Android** devices will be subject to pay extra due to the **App
  Store** or **Google Play** fees.
</Warning>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Server Perks">
    <p>
      Server perks can be obtained by subscribing to Echo through [**Discord's store
      page**](https://discord.com/application-directory/1377953187176251564/store).
      These perks are applied to a specific server and grant benefits to all
      users in that server.
    </p>

    <p>
      <AccordionGroup>
        <Accordion title="Video support">
          Enables support for editing videos for most commands.
        </Accordion>
        <Accordion title="Larger input resolution">
          Increases the maximum input resolution from 512x512 to 1024x1024.
        </Accordion>
        <Accordion title="Longer processing time">
          The time limit for each operation is increased from 40 seconds to 60 seconds.
        </Accordion>
        <Accordion title="GIF frame limit increase">
          The maximum number of frames for GIFs is increased from 150 to 200.
        </Accordion>
      </AccordionGroup>
    </p>
