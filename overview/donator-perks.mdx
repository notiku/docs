## How to obtain Donator Perks

Donator perks can be purchased either through
[**Discord's store page**](https://discord.com/application-directory/1377953187176251564/store) or
[**Patreon**](https://www.patreon.com/notiku).

<Warning>
  **iOS** and **Android** devices will be subject to pay extra due to the **App
  Store** or **Google Play** fees.
</Warning>
